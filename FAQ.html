What Genres do you write in?

I write mostly in Fantasy, but I also write in Mystery and Dystopian as well. My first novel, Alex Lugara, is a fantasy novel. Meanwhile, my second is a dystopian novel, and my third will be an action/mystery novel (if Time Travel is a separate genre it falls under that as well).

Who is/are your favorite author(s)?

Hmm, that is a difficult question. Personally, I read many many novels and novellas, and it's not always the author that means the book is going to be good. Therefore, this question isn't one I feel I can answer, as many of my "favorite authors" have written books that I do not like.

Why make books?

A question that I get surprisingly often, and one that is almost impossible to answer in a short amount of time (or space on a page). I guess the simplest explanation I could give as to why I make books, is that I am a one. In essence, we all are books, or more accurately stories. I have a tattoo of an empty book on my shoulder to remind me that my story isn't over yet, and the reason that I make books is to share stories with people. I am a story, you are a story, and stories are what give everyone emotions. So, I write down stories that I know, stories inside of myself, to share with the world, because one day my last page will come...and why should all of those worlds go unexplored?
